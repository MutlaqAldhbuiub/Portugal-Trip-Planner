Amazing Views

Lisbon - Miradouro da Senhora do Monte

Free

The highest viewpoint in Lisbon offering a breathtaking 250-degree panorama of the city. Less crowded than other viewpoints.

Best time: Sunset for golden hour lighting

Lisbon - Miradouro de Santa Luzia

Free

Charming terrace with views over Alfama and the Tagus River. Features traditional Portuguese tiles and a pergola with wisteria.

Best time: Morning for peaceful atmosphere

Lisbon - Park Bar

Free entry (drinks €5-10)

Rooftop bar on top of a parking garage with spectacular views of the Tagus River and 25 de Abril Bridge. Relaxed atmosphere.

Best time: Late afternoon to evening

Lisbon - Miradouro da Graça

Free

Terrace viewpoint with sweeping views of central Lisbon, São Jorge Castle, and the river. Has a small café.

Best time: Late afternoon

Sintra - Pena Palace Terrace

Included with palace ticket (€10.82)

Spectacular views from the colorful palace terraces overlooking the mountains of Sintra and, on clear days, all the way to the Atlantic.

Best time: Morning to avoid crowds

Sesimbra - Sesimbra Castle

€3

Medieval castle with panoramic views over the town and coastline. Peaceful spot away from crowds.

Best time: Late morning or late afternoon

Cascais - Boca do Inferno Cliff Walk

Free

Dramatic coastal views from the cliff path leading to and around the Boca do Inferno sea arch.

Best time: Late afternoon for golden light

Porto - Dom Luís I Bridge Upper Deck

Free

Spectacular views of Porto and Vila Nova de Gaia from the upper deck of this iconic double-decker bridge.

Best time: Sunset and blue hour

Porto - Jardim do Morro

Free

Garden viewpoint in Vila Nova de Gaia with stunning views of Porto's riverside district. Popular sunset spot with locals.

Best time: Golden hour/sunset

Porto - Miradouro da Vitória

Free

Hidden viewpoint offering spectacular views of the Douro River, Dom Luís I Bridge, and Porto's historic center.

Best time: Morning or late afternoon

Transportation Options

From Lisbon Airport to City Center

  • Metro: €1.65 (plus €0.50 for reusable card)
  • Taxi: €15-20
  • Uber/Bolt: €10-15
  • Airport Bus: €4

Lisbon to Sintra

  • Train: €4.60 round trip (40 minutes)
  • Guided Tour: €60-80

Lisbon to Cascais

  • Train: €4.50 round trip (30 minutes)
  • Guided Tour: €60-80

Lisbon to Sesimbra

  • Bus: €4.50 one way (1 hour)
  • Uber/Bolt: €30-40

Lisbon to Porto

  • Train: €20-35 (3 hours)
  • Bus: €15-25 (3.5 hours)
  • Domestic Flight: €50-80 (1 hour)

Public Transport in Lisbon

  • 24-hour ticket: €6.75
  • Single metro/bus ticket: €1.65
  • Viva Viagem card: €0.50 + top-up
  • Tram 28 ticket: €3.00

Public Transport in Porto

  • 24-hour ticket: €7
  • Single metro/bus ticket: €1.70
  • Andante card: €0.60 + top-up

Safety Tips for Solo Travelers

  • Use official taxis or ride-sharing apps
  • Keep valuables secure, especially in tourist areas
  • Share your itinerary with someone you trust
  • Stay in well-lit, populated areas at night
  • Get a local SIM card for constant connectivity
