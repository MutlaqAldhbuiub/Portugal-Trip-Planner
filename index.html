Recommended Restaurants

Lisbon - Taberna Sal Grosso

€10-15 per meal

Contemporary twists on traditional recipes in a cheerful atmosphere. Try the crispy pork belly or tuna pica-pau.

Location: Alfama district Map

Lisbon - Time Out Market

€15-25 per meal

Food hall featuring top local chefs and restaurants. Great for sampling a variety of Portuguese dishes in one place.

Location: Cais do Sodré Map

Lisbon - Cervejaria Ramiro

€30-40 per meal

Legendary seafood restaurant popular with locals and tourists alike. Famous for giant tiger prawns, crab, and fresh clams.

Location: Avenida Almirante Reis Rua Santa Catarina, Porto Map

Porto - Café Santiago

€10-15 per meal

Home of Porto's iconic Francesinha sandwich - layers of meat, sausage and ham topped with melted cheese and spicy tomato beer sauce.

Location: Rua Passos Manuel, Porto Map

Cascais - Mar do Inferno

€25-40 per meal

Cliffside seafood restaurant with stunning ocean views. Famous for grilled fish, seafood rice, and lobster. Try to book a table with an ocean view.

Location: Boca do Inferno, Cascais Map

Lisbon - A Cevicheria

€20-30 per meal

Modern Peruvian-Portuguese fusion restaurant by Chef Kiko Martins. Known for creative ceviches and pisco sours. Look for the giant octopus hanging from the ceiling!

Location: Príncipe Real, Lisbon Map

Sintra - Incomum

€15-25 per meal

Contemporary restaurant with a seasonal menu featuring Portuguese ingredients with modern twists. Good lunch spot between castle visits with excellent value fixed-price menu.

Location: Centro Histórico, Sintra Map

Porto - Casa Guedes

€5-10 per meal

No-frills sandwich shop famous for their pork roast sandwich with Serra da Estrela cheese. Simple, inexpensive, and absolutely delicious. Expect a line during peak hours.

Location: Praça dos Poveiros, Porto Map

Amazing Views

Lisbon - Miradouro da Senhora do Monte

Free

The highest viewpoint in Lisbon offering a breathtaking 250-degree panorama of the city. Less crowded than other viewpoints.

Best time: Sunset for golden hour lighting Map

Lisbon - Miradouro de Santa Luzia

Free

Romantic terrace with vine-covered pergola and beautiful azulejo tile panels. Panoramic views of Alfama's terracotta rooftops and the Tagus River.

Best time: Morning or late afternoon Map

Sintra - Pena Palace

€10.82

Colorful 19th-century Romanticist castle perched on a hill. Incredible views of the surrounding mountains, forests and even the Atlantic Ocean on clear days.

Best time: Early morning (avoid midday crowds) Map

Porto - Jardim do Morro

Free

Hilltop garden offering spectacular views of Porto's riverside district, the Dom Luis I Bridge, and the port wine cellars of Vila Nova de Gaia.

Best time: Sunset (locals gather with drinks) Map

Cascais - Boca do Inferno

Free

Dramatic sea cliff formation with crashing waves and rugged coastal scenery. The name means "Mouth of Hell" due to the intense way the sea rushes into the cave.

Best time: Late afternoon for best light Map

Lisbon - São Jorge Castle

€10

Historic Moorish castle on one of Lisbon's highest hills offering 360° views of the city. Walk along the defensive walls for different vantage points of the city and river.

Best time: Early morning to avoid crowds Map

Porto - Serra do Pilar

Free (€5 for monastery)

Hilltop viewpoint next to the Serra do Pilar Monastery offering stunning panoramic views of Porto, the Douro River, and the iconic Dom Luis I Bridge.

Best time: Evening blue hour Map

Sintra - Castle of the Moors

€8

Ancient medieval fortress with stone walls snaking across hilltops. Climb to the battlements for breathtaking views of Sintra, Pena Palace, and the Portuguese countryside.

Best time: Late afternoon Map

Porto - Clerigos Tower

€6

Baroque bell tower with 240 steps to the top, offering 360° views of Porto's historic center. One of the city's most recognizable landmarks and worth the climb.

Best time: Late morning or before sunset Map

Transportation Options

From Lisbon Airport to City Center

  • Metro: €1.65 (plus €0.50 for reusable card)
  • Taxi: €15-20
  • Uber/Bolt: €10-15
  • Airport Bus: €4

Lisbon to Porto

  • Alfa Pendular (fast train): €35-45
  • Intercidades (intercity): €25-35
  • Express Bus: €20-30
  • Car Rental: €40-60 per day

Lisbon to Sintra

  • Train from Rossio Station: €2.25 one-way (40 minutes)
  • Train from Oriente Station: €2.25 one-way (47 minutes)
  • Bus 434 Tourist Circuit in Sintra: €8 (hop-on-hop-off)
  • Taxi/Uber within Sintra: €5-10 per ride

Lisbon to Cascais

  • Train from Cais do Sodré: €2.25 one-way (40 minutes)
  • Bus 403 (Cascais-Sintra): €4 one-way (30 minutes)
  • Bike rental in Cascais: €10-15 per day
  • Taxi/Uber from Lisbon: €20-30

Lisbon Public Transport

  • 24-hour Metro/Bus/Tram ticket: €6.60
  • Zapping card (reloadable): €0.50 + balance
  • Historic Tram 28 single ride: €3
  • Lisboa Card (1/2/3 days): €20/34/42

Porto Public Transport

  • Andante card (reloadable): €0.60 + fare
  • 24-hour unlimited travel: €7
  • Porto Card (1/2/3 days): €13/20/25
  • Douro River Cruise: €15-20
