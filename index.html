Recommended Accommodations

Lisbon - The Lumiares Hotel & Spa

€180 per night

Boutique hotel in the trendy Bairro Alto district with spa facilities and rooftop bar. Apartment-style rooms with kitchenettes perfect for solo travelers.

Address: Rua do Diário de Notícias 142, 1200-146 Lisboa Map

Lisbon - Brown's Central Hotel

€120 per night

Recommended Restaurants

Lisbon - Taberna Sal Grosso

€10-15 per meal

Contemporary twists on traditional recipes in a cheerful atmosphere. Try the crispy pork belly or tuna pica-pau.

Location: Alfama district Map

Lisbon - Time Out Market

€15-25 per meal

Food hall featuring top local chefs and restaurants. Great for sampling a variety of Portuguese dishes in one place.

Location: Cais do Sodré Map

Best Coffee Shops

Lisbon - Copenhagen Coffee Lab

€3-5 for coffee, €5-8 for breakfast

Scandinavian-style coffee shop with excellent espresso, pour-overs, and freshly baked pastries. Quiet space good for relaxing with a book.

Location: Multiple locations, including Príncipe Real Map

Amazing Views

Lisbon - Miradouro da Senhora do Monte

Free

The highest viewpoint in Lisbon offering a breathtaking 250-degree panorama of the city. Less crowded than other viewpoints.

Best time: Sunset for golden hour lighting Map
