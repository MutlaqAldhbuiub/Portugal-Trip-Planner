Recommended Restaurants

Lisbon - Taberna Sal Grosso

€10-15 per meal

Contemporary twists on traditional recipes in a cheerful atmosphere. Try the crispy pork belly or tuna pica-pau.

Location: Alfama district

Lisbon - Time Out Market

€15-25 per meal

Food hall featuring top Lisbon eateries with a wide variety of options in one place.

Location: Cais do Sodré

Lisbon - Frade dos Mares

€20-30 per meal

Excellent seafood restaurant specializing in dishes like Polvo à Lagareiro (octopus with olive oil and garlic).

Location: Cais do Sodré

Lisbon - Casa do Alentejo

€20-30 per meal

Located in a historic Moorish-style building, serving specialties from the Alentejo region.

Location: Baixa district

Sintra - Incomum

€15-25 per meal

Fusion restaurant with a creative menu and excellent wine selection.

Location: Sintra town center

Cascais - Mar do Inferno

€25-35 per meal

Renowned seafood restaurant with ocean views and fresh daily catches.

Location: Near Boca do Inferno

Porto - Café Santiago

€10-15 per meal

Famous for the Francesinha, Porto's signature sandwich.

Location: Rua de Passos Manuel

Must-See Attractions

Lisbon - São Jorge Castle

€15 per person

Historic Moorish castle offering panoramic views of Lisbon.

Hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM daily

Lisbon - Jerónimos Monastery

€12.5 per person

UNESCO World Heritage site showcasing Manueline architecture.

Hours: 9:30 AM - 6:00 PM (Closed Mondays)

Lisbon - Belém Tower

€6 per person

16th-century fortress symbolizing Portugal's Age of Discovery.

Hours: 10:00 AM - 6:30 PM (May-Sep), 10:00 AM - 5:30 PM (Oct-Apr)

Lisbon - Oceanário de Lisboa

€25 per person

One of the largest aquariums in the world with diverse marine life.

Hours: 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM daily

Sintra - Pena Palace

€10.82 per person

Vibrant 19th-century Romanticist palace with stunning views.

Hours: 9:30 AM - 6:30 PM daily

Sintra - Quinta da Regaleira

€22.72 per person

Neo-Gothic mansion with mystical gardens, secret tunnels, and the famous Initiation Well.

Hours: 10:00 AM - 6:30 PM daily

Cascais - Boca do Inferno

Free

Dramatic sea arch and collapsed cave with crashing waves.

Hours: Open 24/7

Porto - Clérigos Tower

€6 per person

18th-century Baroque bell tower with panoramic city views.

Hours: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM daily

Transportation Options
